About Pyrotech

We are an elite team of student computer enthusiasts. We have represented the school in various competitions and won laurels in events such as web designing, software development, image editing, movie making, 3-D modelling, photography and others.

'Pyrotech' is our brand name and we are known for our burning desire and passion to promote technology.
